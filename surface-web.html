Surface Web

What is the Surface Web?

The Surface Web is the portion of the internet that is visible and accessible to everyone. It includes public websites that are indexed by search engines like Google, Bing, and Yahoo. This is where most people browse for information, make purchases, access social media, and engage in other everyday activities.

Advantages

  • Easy accessibility for everyone.
  • Search engines index the content, making it easy to find.
  • It is the most commonly used web for education, shopping, entertainment, and communication.

Risks

  • Invasive advertising and data collection by websites.
  • Privacy concerns with tracking and cookies.
  • Can be censored or restricted in some countries.

Dampak (Impact)

The Surface Web has revolutionized the way we access information, but it has also led to issues like information overload and privacy erosion. It is essential to be aware of privacy settings and the information shared while browsing.

How to Access

Simple browsers like Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, or Safari are all you need to access the Surface Web.
